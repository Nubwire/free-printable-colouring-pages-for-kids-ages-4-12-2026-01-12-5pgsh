Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our colouring pages.

Are these colouring pages really free?

Yes, absolutely! All our colouring pages are completely free to download and print. We believe in making creative activities accessible to every family. There are no hidden costs, subscriptions, or premium memberships required. Simply download, print, and start colouring!

What age groups are these colouring pages suitable for?

Our colouring pages are designed for children ages 4 to 12 years old. We organize our designs by age group and complexity level, so you can easily find pages that match your child's developmental stage and interests. Younger children (4-6) have simpler designs with larger areas to colour, while older kids (9-12) enjoy more detailed and intricate patterns.

What's the best way to print these colouring pages?

Our colouring pages are optimized for standard 8.5" x 11" paper (letter size). We recommend printing on regular white paper or cardstock for best results. If you want extra durability, cardstock works wonderfully. You can print in black and white (which uses less ink) or in colour if you prefer. All our designs print clearly and are ready to colour immediately after printing!

Can I use these colouring pages in my classroom or organization?

Absolutely! Teachers, librarians, and community organizations are welcome to use our colouring pages for educational purposes. They're perfect for classroom activities, after-school programs, summer camps, and community events. Simply print as many copies as you need for your students or participants. We just ask that you don't claim them as your own or resell them.

How often are new colouring pages added?

We regularly add new designs to our collection to keep things fresh and exciting! New colouring pages are added monthly, featuring seasonal themes, popular characters, nature designs, and more. Subscribe to our newsletter or check back regularly to discover the latest additions. Your feedback helps us create designs that kids love!

About ColorFun Kids

ColorFun Kids was born from a simple idea: children deserve access to high-quality, engaging creative activities that don't require a screen. Founded by a team of parents and educators who believed in the power of hands-on creativity, we set out to create a resource that families everywhere could enjoy without cost or complexity. What started as a small collection of designs has grown into a comprehensive library of beautifully illustrated colouring pages that inspire imagination and artistic expression in children of all ages. We're passionate about making screen-free fun accessible to every family, regardless of their budget.

Our mission is to spark creativity, foster a love of art, and provide parents with quality screen-free activities that keep children engaged and happy. We believe that every child deserves the opportunity to explore their artistic potential, and that parents should have access to trusted, safe, and enriching resources. We're committed to continuously improving our collection, listening to feedback from families and educators, and creating designs that reflect the diverse interests and developmental needs of children. At ColorFun Kids, we're not just providing colouring pages — we're building a community of families who value creativity, imagination, and meaningful time together.
